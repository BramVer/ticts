Operations
----------

Operations can be done either on one value, or on another TimeSeries object.

They return another TimeSeries object.

Here is a (non-exhaustive) list of the available operations:


+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| Name                 | Example Usage one value | Example Usage TimeSeries |
+======================+=========================+==========================+
| addition             | ts + 10                 | ts1 + ts2                |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| recursive addition   | sum([ts, 1, 10])        | sum([ts1, ts2, ts3])     |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| subtraction          | ts - 5                  | ts1 - ts2                |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| multiplication       | ts * 1000               | ts1 * ts2                |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| division             | ts / 1000               | ts1 / ts2                |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| absolute value       | abs(ts)                 |                          |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| lower than           | ts < 10                 | ts1 < ts2                |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| greater than         | ts > 10                 | ts1 > ts2                |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| equals               | ts == 10                | ts1 == ts2               |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| or / and / xor / not | not ts                  | ts1 or ts2               |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| floor                | ts.floor(20)            | ts1.floor(ts2)           |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
| ceil                 | ts.ceil(4)              | ts1.ceil(ts2)            |
+----------------------+-------------------------+--------------------------+
